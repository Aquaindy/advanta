Reports

One click turns everything your AI growth team did over a window into a clean, shareable roll-up — agent activity, recommendations, campaigns, SEO, landing pages, A/B tests and more — as PDF or CSV.

Daily · Weekly · Monthly PDF & CSV export Real data only

One sidebar page: Reports. Reports pull real workspace data — they never invent numbers, and only show the sections you actually have data for.

⚡ 60-second quickstart

Reports → click Daily, Weekly, or Monthly → open the generated report → Download PDF or Download CSV.

1Generate a report — /reports

  1. Pick a window — Daily (last 24 hours), Weekly (last 7 days), or Monthly (last 30 days).
  2. It rolls up real records across the window into a single report.
  3. Open it from the list (with its period, generated time, and status), then export.

Generating requires the Marketer role or higher; anyone on the workspace can view and download.

3Export & share

  • PDF — a clean, client-ready document of the full report.
  • CSV — the tabular data for your own spreadsheets/BI.
  • Reports are saved in the list, so you build a history of snapshots over time.

4Empty states

No data, no fabrication

A report only includes sections you have real data for. A brand-new workspace's report will be sparse — connect integrations, run agents, and audit pages first, then generate. That's the no-fake-data rule working as intended.

?FAQ & tips

My report is mostly empty.

It reflects what's in the window. Run some agents, sync campaigns, audit a landing page, then regenerate — the relevant sections fill in.

Which window should I use?

Daily for standups, Weekly for team reviews, Monthly for executive/client snapshots.

Can I get campaign spend & ROAS here?

Paid-ads sections summarize your synced campaigns. For live, drill-down ad metrics use the Ads module; for traffic-source ROI use the Traffic dashboard.

Where do the readiness scores come from?

From your Growth DNA profile — derived from onboarding, not from ad data.
