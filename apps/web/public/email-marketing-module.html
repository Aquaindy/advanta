⚡ 60-second quickstart

Autoresponders → connect Omnisend with your API key → Email campaigns → Sync now → Run audit → read your score & the 7-section report. Push ad leads into a tag any time from the Autoresponders page.

1Connect an autoresponder — /autoresponders

Go to Autoresponders (sidebar → Advertising). Each provider shows a card with its status. To connect, paste your API key (plus any config fields) and click Connect — AdGenieHQ validates the key and pulls your account name.

  • Keys are encrypted at rest; the browser never sees the raw token.
  • Connecting / disconnecting requires the Admin role and is audit-logged.
  • A connected card shows Connected · Last sync and any last error.

Two honest limits to know

1) Email-campaign analytics are pulled from Omnisend today — GetResponse & custom are for contact sync. 2) Omnisend's API is tag-based: it can't create automations for you. You build the automation once in Omnisend, and AdGenieHQ tags the right contacts to trigger it.

3Push & pull contacts — two-way sync

On a connected provider's card, use the Sync contacts panel:

  1. Pick the audience — choose a list (GetResponse) or type a tag (Omnisend / custom).
  2. Push to list — paste contacts (one per line: email, First, Last) to send ad leads into your ESP.
  3. Pull from list — fetch subscribers back into AdGenieHQ (up to 100 shown inline).
  4. Check the activity feed — every push/pull is logged with succeeded / partial / failed counts and timestamps.

Partial failures are recorded honestly (e.g. 90/100 succeeded = "partial"), never silently dropped.

4Sync email campaigns — /email

Open Email campaigns and click Sync now to pull every Omnisend send with full engagement & deliverability metrics:

Tracked per campaign

Bounce rate over 2% is flagged red so list-hygiene problems jump out.

5AI email audit — "Run audit"

Click Run audit to run the email_marketing agent. It scores your program out of 100 and returns a 7-section report:

  1. Audit & score (/100) — graded Excellent / Healthy / Needs work / At risk, with subscores for open, click, bounce, complaint, and unsubscribe health.
  2. Segments — heuristic segments (lapsed / never-opened, engaged clickers/VIP, opened-not-clicked) plus AI suggestions.
  3. Black Friday campaign draft — AI-written subject lines, hook, and CTA (deterministic template if no LLM).
  4. Open-rate trend — month-by-month series with deltas and an up/down/flat direction.
  5. Best send day — open rate by day-of-week with the top day ranked.
  6. Subject-line patterns — lift from emoji, personalization, numbers, questions, urgency, and length, plus your top 5 subjects.
  7. Deliverability — verdict (healthy / at-risk) with flags for complaint, bounce, and unsubscribe red lines.

Straight about the data

The report says what it can't see: Omnisend's API has no recipient country or hour-of-day (so "best send day" is day-of-week only), and deliverability is based on reputation signals (complaints/bounces), not seed-inbox testing or SPF/DKIM/DMARC checks. No campaigns with send/open data yet? The audit returns a clean "nothing to audit" state.

6Link email ↔ paid ads

On each email campaign row, pick a linked paid-ads campaign from the dropdown (or "Not linked"). This ties email engagement to the ad campaign that drove the leads, for cross-channel reporting. Select "Not linked" to unlink. The change is audit-logged.

7Suggested email copy

From your Growth DNA Profile, the Content Studio can generate ready-to-use copy — including email (lifecycle / nurture) drafts alongside ad copy, landing pages, social posts, and more. Find these under Creatives / suggested copies, filter by the email type, and download as TXT or DOCX.

?FAQ & tips

I connected Omnisend but see no campaigns.

Connecting only stores the key. Go to Email campaigns → Sync now to pull your sends. The page starts empty by design — no fake data.

Can AdGenieHQ send the emails for me?

The module syncs campaign results from your ESP and tags contacts to trigger automations you build there. It doesn't replace your ESP's send engine — it makes your existing program measurable and adds an AI audit on top.

Which provider should I use for the audit?

Use Omnisend — campaign analytics (and therefore the audit) are pulled from Omnisend today. GetResponse and custom webhooks are for contact push/pull.

Does the audit need an OpenAI key?

No. The score, trends, send-day, subject patterns, and deliverability are deterministic. A key just enriches the executive summary, segment ideas, and Black Friday draft.

How do I move ad leads into email follow-up?

Push them to an Omnisend tag from the Autoresponders page, then trigger an Omnisend automation — or plan the whole flow in Traffic Genie → Omnisend journeys.
