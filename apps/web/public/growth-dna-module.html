⚡ 60-second quickstart

Onboarding → fill 5 steps → Generate Growth DNA Profile → you land on Growth DNA with scores + strategy in under a second (AI polish streams in right after). Revisit onboarding any time and regenerate.

1The onboarding wizard — /onboarding

Five sequential steps. Required fields are marked; everything else sharpens the output but is optional.

  1. Business basics — name, website, industry.
  2. Audience & offer — who you sell to, what you sell, top pain points.
  3. Goals & budget — primary conversion goal, monthly ad-budget range, geographic targets.
  4. Channels & analytics — ad platforms in use, landing-page URLs, analytics status, competitors.
  5. Brand voice — the tone your copy should match.

3Generating your Growth DNA

The final step's button — Generate Growth DNA Profile — builds your profile in two layers:

  1. Instant, deterministic core — scores and every section are computed from your answers in under a second and saved immediately. You're never left waiting.
  2. AI polish (background) — if an LLM is configured, it rewrites the creative parts (strategy prose, hooks, content calendar) and the page live-updates from "pending" to "enriched." No key? You keep the full deterministic version.

Always real, never blocked

Required fields are validated first, the deterministic engine always returns a complete profile, and AI enrichment is purely additive — if it fails, your profile is untouched.

5The marketing strategy — a full playbook

The profile also includes a cross-channel strategy bundle:

  • Overview — a thesis, top priorities, and a suggested budget allocation across channels.
  • Channel strategies grouped into Paid / Owned / Earned / Measurement — each with priority, status, cadence, tactics, KPIs, and a concrete first step.
  • Content pillars with an 80/20 split and example hooks.
  • Platform strategy table — cadence and best-for per platform.
  • Email & lifecycle — newsletter cadence, automation flows, and KPIs.
  • 30-day content calendar — day, channel, format, pillar, and a hook direction.

?FAQ & tips

Can I change my answers later?

Yes — reopen Onboarding, update anything, and regenerate your Growth DNA. Downstream agents pick up the new context on their next run.

Do I need an OpenAI/Anthropic key?

No. The deterministic engine produces a complete, useful profile on its own. A key just adds AI-polished prose, hooks, and a filled content calendar.

Where do the scores come from?

They're computed from your onboarding inputs (e.g. budget ≥ $500, analytics configured, landing pages provided) — not from connected ad accounts. They measure how ready you are, not current performance.

Who can generate it?

Generating the Growth DNA and editing onboarding requires the Marketer role or higher; anyone on the workspace can view the result.
