# AdVanta — 2-Minute Interactive Dashboard Demo Script

**Format:** screen-recorded product walkthrough with voiceover.
**Duration:** ~2:00 (≈ 280 words of VO @ 140 WPM).
**Audience:** founders, in-house growth marketers, and agencies evaluating AdVanta.
**Tone:** calm, executive, confident — never hype. Match the brand voice
("Premium, Calm, Executive, Intelligent").

> **Producer notes**
> - Use a real demo workspace with at least one connected provider (GA4 +
>   Google Ads is enough). No fabricated metrics on screen.
> - Cursor moves should be slow and deliberate. No fast click-through.
> - On-screen captions in **bold purple** match the Grape Jelly brand.
> - Background music: low ambient pad, –18 LUFS so VO sits cleanly on top.
> - Final CTA card stays on screen for 3 full seconds before fade.

---

## Scene 1 — Hook (0:00 → 0:10)

**On screen:** AdVanta logomark fades in on cloud-white background → quick
cut to the Command Center dashboard, lit but not loud.

**Caption:** Your growth command center.

**VO:**
> "If your ad ops live across five tabs and a backlog of optimizations no
> one has time for, this is for you. AdVanta runs the growth playbook
> with you — read your accounts, surface waste, ship fixes."

---

## Scene 2 — Connect once, every agent reads (0:10 → 0:30)

**On screen:** Cursor lands on the **Integrations** tab. Show real OAuth
connection cards (Google Ads, Meta Ads, LinkedIn Ads, GA4, GSC) with green
"Connected" pills. Cursor moves to the **Command Center** — KPI tiles light
up: *Connected accounts*, *Active campaigns*, *Open recs*, *Spend today*.

**Caption:** One connect. Eleven agents.

**VO:**
> "Connect Google, Meta, LinkedIn, GA4, and Search Console once. Every
> agent reads from the same data. Tokens are encrypted at rest. Nothing
> ever touches your browser."

---

## Scene 3 — A real recommendation (0:30 → 0:55)

**On screen:** Click **Recommendations**. List of cards: *Pause stale
campaign · projected $1,240 monthly waste · high confidence*. Cursor opens
the card. Show the agent's reasoning panel — data behind the call, the
exact platform write planned, expected impact.

**Caption:** Every recommendation, fully traceable.

**VO:**
> "The Paid Ads agent caught a campaign live past its end-date — burning
> twelve hundred and forty dollars a month. Here's the data behind the
> call, the exact change AdVanta will make on Google Ads, and the
> projected impact. Approve, edit, or reject."

---

## Scene 4 — Approve → execute → audit (0:55 → 1:20)

**On screen:** Click **Approve**. Confirmation modal: *"Apply on Google
Ads — pause campaign · revert available."* Click **Confirm**. Toast: *"Pause
executed · saved to audit log."* Status pill flips to **Applied**. Cursor
hovers the **Audit log** entry — one line: *User · approved · paused
campaign #4421 · diff captured*.

**Caption:** Approval-gated. Reversible. Logged.

**VO:**
> "The change goes live in seconds. Prior state captured, so any single
> write is reversible. Every action — User, Agent, System — lands in the
> audit log with the full diff."

---

## Scene 5 — Autopilot, on your terms (1:20 → 1:40)

**On screen:** Navigate to **Autopilot**. Show the guardrail panel:
max daily budget shift, stop-loss, action allowlist, minimum confidence.
Toggle Autopilot to **On**. The "what would auto-approve" preview shows
two recommendations turning green; one stays human-routed because it
exceeds the budget cap.

**Caption:** Automation, with the rails you set.

**VO:**
> "When you're ready, flip Autopilot on. Pick the actions agents can run,
> set your stop-loss, cap your max budget shift. Anything outside the
> rails still routes to you."

---

## Scene 6 — Reports + close (1:40 → 2:00)

**On screen:** Click **Reports**. Show daily/weekly/monthly tabs, then a
quick PDF preview. Cut to the marketing landing page hero. Final card:
AdVanta logo + tagline + URL.

**Caption:** Try it free for fourteen days.

**VO:**
> "Daily, weekly, and monthly reports are built from your real data. No
> fabricated numbers. Connect one account, queue your first
> recommendation in minutes. AdVanta — your growth command center."

## Style + voice cheatsheet

| Do | Don't |
|---|---|
| Show real account data with numbers blurred where needed | Use stock dashboards or screenshots from competitors |
| Use action verbs: "approve", "ship", "execute", "audit" | "Synergize", "transform", "revolutionize" |
| Pause for 0.5s after key claims | Speak over fast cuts |
| Read prices in full ("ninety-nine dollars per month") | Skip prices to seem premium — clarity wins |
| Show one mistake the agent caught | List capabilities without proof |

## Asset checklist (for the producer)

- [ ] Demo workspace seeded with one real ad account (Google Ads + GA4)
- [ ] Pre-recorded "stale campaign" recommendation queued and visible
- [ ] Audit log primed with three recent entries (User, Agent, System)
- [ ] Autopilot config saved with at least one allowed action type
- [ ] Reports tab has at least one rendered weekly PDF available for preview
- [ ] Landing page hero captured at 1080p, 30fps for closing shot
- [ ] AdVanta brand assets (logomark SVG, Grape Jelly hex `#3E2F84`)
- [ ] VO recorded in a treated room, –20 LUFS, breaths edited out
- [ ] Captions burned in for silent autoplay on social

---

## Optional 60-second cutdown

If a one-minute cut is needed for paid social, drop scenes 2 and 5 and
shorten 1 and 4 by half. Keep the core arc: **problem → real
recommendation → approval → audit → CTA**. That's the load-bearing
sequence; everything else is supporting detail.
